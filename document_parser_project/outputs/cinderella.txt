Cinderella
Cinderella lived with her stepmother and two stepsisters.
They treated her badly and made her do all the housework.
A royal ball was announced by the prince.
Cinderella wanted to go but was not allowed.
Her fairy godmother helped her with magic.
She got a beautiful dress and glass slippers.
The magic would end at midnight.
At the ball, the prince danced with Cinderella.
She ran away at midnight and lost a slipper.
The prince searched for the girl who fit the slipper.
The slipper fit Cinderella perfectly.
Cinderella married the prince and lived happily ever after
